About Us

Welcome to Kays, St Germain & Co., PLLC (KS), a full service CPA firm specializing in providing exceptional tax, accounting and consulting services to closely held businesses and individuals throughout Northwest Minnesota and beyond.

We work hard to build close relationships with our clients, gaining an understanding of their goals and unique needs. We take time to get to know them and then provide ideas that work.

The professionals at KS have over forty years of experience delivering innovative, yet practical solutions for clients. KS offers the service and attention you expect from a smaller firm, with the expertise you expect from a larger firm.

Our purpose is to serve and meet the accounting and tax needs of our clients in a professional manner. Having professional staff on hand ensures you the quality that you should expect when doing business with an accounting firm.
