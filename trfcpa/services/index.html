Services

TAX SERVICES

General Tax Planning

Both individuals and businesses can benefit from being proactive. Tax planning starts with getting to know you and understanding your goals and challenges. We’ve helped save clients thousands of tax dollars by knowing their individual and business tax situations well enough to recognize when opportunities for tax saving exist.

Tax Preparation

Federal and State tax compliance becomes more complicated every year. Our tax professionals refer to the latest tax law changes when preparing your federal, state, and/or multistate tax returns.

IRS and State Representation

Receiving a notice from federal or state tax authorities can a stressful experience. When you hire our firm to prepare your tax return, our service doesn’t end there. Our tax professionals will review the notice and help you understand what the federal and state authorities want and inform you of your options. We work with you until the matter is resolved.

ACCOUNTING SERVICES

Bookkeeping Services

Accurate record-keeping is essential to a successful business yet can also be complicated and time consuming. We can help you with the organization of day-to-day tasks of bookkeeping so you can focus more on running your business. We can customize a package of services to perform for your business on a monthly, quarterly or annual basis. Our accounting services offer a wide variety of solutions including, but not limited to:

  • Sales tax reporting
  • Accounts payable and receivable processing
  • Bank statement reconciliation
  • Financial statement processing
  • Cash flow analysis

Review and Compilation Services

Whether your business requires a review or compilation of your financial statements, we are here to help. Our CPAs will work with you and your staff to get the information we need to prepare your financial statements while complying with current reporting practices.

PAYROLL SERVICES

Our payroll services can be customized to your specific needs, from payroll preparation to payroll reporting. When you work with our professionals, you can relax knowing your payroll is taken care of and your taxes are being paid accurately and on time. Our payroll services are tailored to your specific needs and include:

  • Computing and writing payroll checks
  • Direct Deposit
  • Calculating and filing payroll taxes
  • Processing quarterly payroll tax returns
  • Processing and submitting form W-2s.
  • Assisting with providing data for workers’ compensation audits
  • Assisting with government notices

BUSINESS ADVISORY SERVICES

Business Consulting

Our professionals go beyond tax and audit work to help you identify, develop, implement and manage financial strategies that grow the value of your business. We take a look at all aspects of your business to determine the best strategy. We help plan for what may affect you in the short or long term to develop solutions that center on your vision and goals.
