Cheers and Jeers for February 25

Posted February 22, 2018 at 3:30 PM

By Damian Holbrook, TV Insider

Cheers to Netflix for its winning play. The Philadelphia Eagles' victory wasn't the only Super Bowl surprise, thanks to the streaming net's decision to drop The Cloverfield Paradox--Part 3 of J.J. Abrams's monster-movie franchise, starring Gugu Mbatha-Raw--right after the big game wrapped. After all, Patriots fans needed something to distract them from their crushing loss.

Cheers to 9-1-1 for making the best casting call. It's so fitting that Underground alum Aisha Hinds is now playing heroic firefighter Hen Wilson on Fox's first responder drama because our first response to seeing her on screen is always relief that someone else realizes how damn good she is!

Jeers to The Bachelor for Arie's bland ambition. After weeks of whining that cookie-cutter dullard Lauren B. was distant, the race-car romeo chose her for his hometown dates over dynamic, sexy and so-into-him Seinne. Now we know why he's still single!

Jeers to Amazon for being a tease. Hey guys, if you can figure out same-day delivery for Prime items, how come we have to wait until August for your splashy Jack Ryan drama? John Krasinski signed on ages ago, and that big ol' Super Bowl spot looked like he's ready for action right now!
