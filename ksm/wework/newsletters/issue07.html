12 pieces of advice for aspiring veteran entrepreneurs
In the Veterans in Residence program, a partnership between WeWork and Bunker Labs, a national network of veteran entrepreneurs focused on growing its community, a select group of veterans is selected to join a six-month startup incubator and leadership program that provides community, business support, and workspace to help veteran entrepreneurs launch and grow their businesses. This Veterans Day, we asked some of our veterans in residence to share advice for aspiring veteran entrepreneurs. Here’s what they had to say.

Red Hat
Multiple cities around the globe
A software giant taps into a world of agile office space
Red Hat provides enterprise open source technology solutions to companies around the world. Its team of more than 14,000 associates is distributed across 35 countries. Often, Red Hat needs to scale up and down spaces—fast—in various locations, depending on their projects. All the while, Red Hat seeks to give employees flexibility to work from offices that are conveniently located, where they can collaborate with colleagues, and in which they can do their best work. That’s where WeWork comes in.
