When companies take on an 'age is just a number' mentality, everybody wins
New York Times best-selling author Lindsay Pollak explores how businesses benefit when they embrace a multigenerational workforce in her new book, The Remix: How to Lead and Succeed in the Multigenerational Workplace. In her research, Pollak found that companies that actively worked to buck the young talent trend—think hoodies and ping-pong tables—had an advantage in being able to relate to clients and talent across generations as well as innovate and problem-solve.
