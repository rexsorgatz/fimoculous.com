These startups are hard at work battling COVID-19
Diagnostic test kits. Collaboration tools that make remote meetings easier and more secure. A kid-friendly math learning game. These are just some of the innovative ideas coming out of WeWork Labs, a global community of startups that tackle challenges brought forth by the novel coronavirus, COVID-19. See how six innovation teams around the world are working to make life easier and safer for us all right now and into the future.
MEMBER SPOTLIGHT
Highspot
London, England
As the Highspot team in Seattle grew from 150 to 400 employees, the company quickly outgrew their office space. Their quick solve was to obtain a second space across town—a move that proved to be logistically complicated. That’s where WeWork came in, providing office space blocks away from headquarters and streamlining workflow and commutes for the entire staff. When Highspot decided to open a London office, they chose a WeWork space there. In both locations, Highspot was able to customize their space, requesting that walls be taken down for collaboration and that carpeting be put in for softer acoustics. “WeWork’s flexibility, ease of doing business, and well-designed spaces are all attractive to a company experiencing the kind of hypergrowth we’re going through,” says Eric Hollenbeck, Highspot’s senior director of IT, applications, and facilities. It’s been so easy that now the company is looking forward to further expansion in the near future—with WeWork as a partner every step of the way.
