ISSUE 13 • Feb 4, 2019
Beverages made by WeWork members, serving WeWork buildings
The WeWork community is made up of a network of businesses that support one another. Take WeWork’s recent initiative to roll out beverages beyond the coffee and alcohol we originally offered. Driven by changing member preferences, many locations now offer drinks like seltzer, nitro tea, and kombucha. Several of these beverages are made by companies based in the same city (or even building) they’ll be served in. This is the kind of community WeWork members value most: They don’t just want to be people who do what they love; they want to support others who do the same.
MEMBER SPOTLIGHT
Li & Fung
Shanghai, China
Li & Fung’s office was overcrowded. The 100-year-old supply chain giant handled huge amounts of product every day, but they weren't able to do so in a systematic way. The forward-thinking company was hoping to transform for the future, but to do so, they needed to upgrade their regional headquarters in Shanghai. In just three months, the WeWork team created a logistics-handling solution and workspace for the company. The new space features touches of the brand identity, technology embedded throughout meeting rooms, and furniture straight from Li & Fung’s own catalog.
