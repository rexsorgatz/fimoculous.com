Careers are not linear—and that’s OK
Oscar Rodríguez, global coworking spaces specialist at Shopify, was on a stable career path at a large hospitality company when he was approached with an opportunity in a new, fast-growing industry—one in which he had no experience. “What do you do when presented with diverging roads in your career?” he asks. “The first road is familiar, a traditional path to elevated titles and more responsibilities. The other road is windy and veers off into new, unforeseen directions.” He eventually took the plunge and accepted the new role; in the process, he learned the market value of his skills and how to rethink his career goals.
