10 tips to manage stress as you return to the office
Heading back to the workplace may stir up a bunch of conflicting emotions: joy in reuniting with coworkers yet stress over whether to take the elevator or the stairs. Thrive Global, a WeWork member in New York City, offers these research-backed “microsteps” that will help ease your anxiety and boost your mental resilience. These are small, simple actions that anyone can do to help manage their own stress, communicate to teammates with empathy, and redefine productivity as we all navigate this unprecedented time.


MEMBER SPOTLIGHT
Tier
Berlin
Micro-mobility startup Tier, which rents out electric scooters, is on a mission to change mobility for good. But for a company built on the idea of efficient commuting, its suburban office space in the south of Berlin was difficult for many employees to get to. Employees called for a more centrally located headquarters, one that was more inspiring and exciting. That’s when Tier decided on a space in the famous Renzo Piano–designed WeWork Atrium Tower in central Berlin for one of its headquarters. With the new commute, employees were able to save precious time during their days, which led to an uptick in their overall happiness and also their productivity at work.
