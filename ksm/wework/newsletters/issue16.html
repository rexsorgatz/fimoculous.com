ISSUE 16 • March 16, 2020
What Rebel Girls CEO Elena Favilli does all day
Four years ago, Elena Favilli had an idea for a children’s bedtime storybook about incredible women. Upon the success of that New York Times–bestselling idea, she launched a thriving brand comprised of more books, a podcast, and development of products like toys and apparel aimed at inspiring young girls in the same way. She shares insight into her creative process, why she reserves mornings for meetings, and how she manages frequent work travel without getting run down.
MEMBER SPOTLIGHT
NatWest Ventures
London, England
Working and growing at a breakneck pace, the agile Ventures group, which incubates ideas and scales up new digital businesses on behalf of NatWest bank, needed a workplace that would foster innovation—and a conventional office wasn't going to cut it. Sidestepping any traditional bank vibes, the six businesses under the NatWest Ventures portfolio moved into a private floor at WeWork 8 Devonshire Square. The Ventures group collaborated with WeWork to create a flexible floor plan that houses numerous spots for spontaneous breakout sessions, informal meetings, and small group gatherings—with the security and compliance necessary for their industry.
